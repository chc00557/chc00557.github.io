Three Goals

  1. Network
  2. Create
  3. Experience

Express Your Creativity

Have any creative ideas for a business? Do you feel like you were meant to be your own boss? Join SOE and begin your career as an entrepreneur today! Many members have started promoting their business if they have not already done so! If you already have thought of a unique business idea, take your toughts to the Accelerator - a program to compete with other students. The winner gets a cash prize and gained attention to their idea!

Meet New People

Networking is important in the organization. All members and newcomers are welcome to come and share their ideas. Any questions or needed advice is normal and encouraged!

Socialize

SOE has multiple meetings, guest speakers, and socil events. Every meeting includes a free meal that varies from Chick-fil-a to Papa John's. Our guest speakers include many succesfull entrepreneurs who share their story of how they started and the challenges the faced. Meet guest such as Lil' Ice Cream Dude, a young man of 13 who has created his own business here in Athens. Business aside, SOE also offers many social nights. If you like Braves games, traveling, going to Fuzzy's downtown, or even bowling with the business fraternity, SOE frequently has social events.
